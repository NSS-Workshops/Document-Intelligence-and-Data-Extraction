**Document intelligence** is a set of techniques to extract information from documents. The goal is to convert unstructured or semi-structred text into a structured, usable format. This transformation reduces or eliminates the manual effort required to review documents such as sales invoices or contracts, helping streamline business processes. Manual processing is slow, expensive, and prone to error.

In many organizations, critical information is locked inside PDFs, scans, and emails. Document intelligence makes that information accessible and usable.

Document intelligence enables:  
* Automation of routine workflows
* Analytics and reporting from previously inaccessible data
* Compliance and auditing through improved record accessibility  
* Operational efficiency and cost savings

Document intelligence can involve a variety of techniques, including text classification, summarization, and sentiment analysis. In this course, however, we will focus primarily on **data extraction**, the process of extracting values or fields from documents. For example, finding the invoice number or total amount from an invoice would fall under data extraction.
